Where are they located? Info

  • Martensson A
    In front of the central cantine, old and full of historic sights. It is where most of party and music venues are situated.
  • Martensson B
    On the uphill close to the Martensson A, with lots of green spaces, a quiet place for studyng.
  • Chiodo 1
    Another neighbourhood next to central cantine, the old yards and sheds are hotspots for people who lived there.
  • Chiodo 2
    Consists of new developments built to extend the campus, between the central cantine and the theatre one.
  • Maisonettes
    Next to cantine of economics. Old and full of different cultures, lot of the area has been redeveloped in recent years.
  • Monaci
    Quiet and peaceful, close to central cantine, were Jesse used to live.
  • San Gennaro
    It is near to filorosso, polifunzionale and economics cantine.
  • Molicelle A
    Between central and economics cantine, it is home to an extremely diverse mix of nationalities and cultures.
  • Molicelle B
    As Molicelle A is now a lively, young and multicultural neighbourhood.
  • Nervoso
    The farthest, for people do not like to study.
